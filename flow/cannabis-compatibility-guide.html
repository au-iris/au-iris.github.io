Understanding the Basics

What Makes Strains Different?

Cannabis strains have two main ingredients that affect how they work:

  • THC - This creates the "high" feeling. More THC = stronger effects
  • CBD - This is calming and doesn't make you high. It can help balance THC

What are Terpenes?

Terpenes are natural scents in plants that also change how cannabis works:

  • Myrcene (earthy smell) ‚Üí Makes you sleepy and relaxed
  • Limonene (citrus smell) ‚Üí Gives you energy and lifts your mood
  • Linalool (flowery smell) ‚Üí Calms anxiety
  • Pinene (pine smell) ‚Üí Helps you focus and stay alert
