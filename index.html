What is I.R.I.S.?

I.R.I.S. is a simple prompt that helps AI consider knowledge from different cultures and traditions when answering your questions.

Most AI systems learn primarily from English sources and Western academic texts. I.R.I.S. reminds them to also draw on wisdom from around the world.

Why use it?

Different cultures have spent thousands of years developing sophisticated understandings of health, consciousness, relationships, and problem-solving.

I.R.I.S. helps you get responses that include these diverse perspectives alongside modern Western approaches - giving you more complete, nuanced answers.

Example: Ask about managing stress, and instead of only getting CBT techniques, you might also learn about Buddhist mindfulness practices, Taoist breathing methods, or Indigenous grounding rituals - all equally valuable approaches.

How to use I.R.I.S.

  1. Copy the I.R.I.S. prompt
  2. Paste it at the start of any AI conversation
  3. Ask your questions normally
  4. Get more balanced, globally-informed responses

That's it. The AI will now consider multiple cultural perspectives when helping you.

Works on most A.I Engines

I.R.I.S. works with ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and other AI assistants. Just paste it before you start your conversation.
